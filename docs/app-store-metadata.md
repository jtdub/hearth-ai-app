## App Store Description

Hearth AI brings powerful AI chat to your device — completely private, completely offline.

**100% On-Device AI**
Run large language models directly on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac using Apple's Metal GPU acceleration. Your conversations never leave your device.

**No Account Required**
No sign-ups, no subscriptions, no cloud. Just download a model and start chatting.

**Browse & Download Models**
Explore a curated selection of open-source models from Hugging Face Hub, or browse the full catalog. Choose models that fit your device's capabilities — from lightweight 0.5B models to powerful 3B+ models.

**Smart Document Q&A**
Import documents and ask questions about them. Hearth AI chunks and indexes your documents locally for fast, relevant answers — all without sending your data anywhere.

**Conversation Memory**
Hearth AI remembers important details across conversations using on-device memory, making your experience more personal over time.

**Share Extension**
Share text and links from any app directly to Hearth AI for processing.

**Siri & Shortcuts**
Integrate Hearth AI into your workflows with App Intents support for Siri and Shortcuts.

**Your Data, Your Device**
- Zero data collection
- No analytics or telemetry
- No cloud processing
- Works fully offline after downloading a model

## Review Notes

Hearth AI is a local AI chat application. Model files are neural network weight data (GGUF format) downloaded from Hugging Face Hub. These are not executable code. All inference runs on-device using the open-source llama.cpp library via Metal GPU acceleration. No user data leaves the device. The app is fully functional on launch — it guides users to download a model to get started. No account or sign-in is required.

## Privacy Nutrition Label
